Manipal Academy of Higher Education Engineering Test
Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) conducts its own online entrance exam to offer admissions to candidates in the programmes that it has on offer. Candidates can secure admissions in courses provided at Manipal University on the basis of their rank/score in Manipal Entrance Test (MET). The exam, was earlier, known as Manipal University Online Entrance Exam (MU-OET).
